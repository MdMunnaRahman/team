Our Vision

To become a center of excellence in Computer Science and Engineering through quality teaching, learning and transformation of research that contributes to the betterment of society.


Our Mission

  • To produce competent Diploma in Engineering with sound theoretical knowledge and practical skills in the fields of Computer Science and Engineering through a true outcome-based education.
  • To promote quality research that helps in solving real-world problems, thereby contributing to the betterment of humanity.
  • To disseminate values and ideals among the students for upholding professional ethics and morals.
  • To facilitate academia-industry-government collaboration for building the potentiality of life-long learning and solving new challenges.
